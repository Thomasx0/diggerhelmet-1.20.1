Hey diggers! Tired of digging in the dark, with torches constantly going out? Need to hold something other than a torch in your left hand? The Digger's Helmet is your solution!

Here's how to get it:

    Find a zombie that spawns with an Eternal Candle in its hand (20% chance).

    Defeat it, claim the Eternal Candle, and craft the Digger's Helmet.

Features:

    Dynamic lighting when worn (enables dynamic entity lighting on Sodium).

    Custom animated model — watch the candle glow! 🔥

Bonus modules included:

    Auto-Hydrator Module — No more manual drinking! Automatically detects water bottles, buckets, or any liquid containers in your inventory and hydrates you when installed in the helmet.

    Mining Speed Module — Boosts mining speed for pickaxes and shovels when equipped in the Digger's Helmet.

Now dig smarter, not harder! 💡⛏️
